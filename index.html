16-Year Record

The AI Workforce Timeline

Key inflection points that reshaped labour markets from 2010 to 2026

2010
The Pre-AI Era
ML confined to academia. Automation risk 69.5%. AI intensity just 15.8%. Most organisations have no AI strategy.
2012
Deep Learning Ignites
AlexNet wins ImageNet. Deep learning goes mainstream. Universities launch AI programmes. Investment begins climbing.
2015
National AI Strategies Emerge
Global AI investment hits $2.5B. Countries publish national AI roadmaps. Education adoption reaches 3–5% in leading nations.
2018
Augmentation Overtakes Displacement
Automation risk drops to 56% as AI begins working alongside humans. AI intensity crosses 32%. The narrative begins to shift.
2020
Remote Work Accelerates AI Adoption
COVID reshapes how work operates. AI job postings reach 7,378. Python appears in 47.5% of all AI roles worldwide.
2022
The Generative AI Explosion
ChatGPT launches. LLM Engineer, RAG Engineer and AI Agent Developer emerge as job titles. AI intensity surges to 45.9%.
2024
AI Becomes Core Infrastructure
LLM Engineer demand score: 98/100. AI Solutions Architects earn $251K/yr. Education adoption crosses 49% globally.
2026
Now — The Question Is Readiness
AI intensity at 48.9%. Education adoption projected 60%+. The workforce transformation is no longer coming — it is here.
